Three Albums

  • Rubber Soul (1965) - A major shift in the sound of the band, Rubber Soul showcased the evolving maturity of The Beatles music
  • Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band (1967) - Reportedly taking over 700 hours to record, this album was filled with brilliant experimentation with concepts that had never been tried in music
  • The White Album (1968) - This album is credited with beginning the breakup of the Beatles but it continued to show theevolution of the sound. This album also began to show styles distinct to each member of the band that would be carried into their solo careers
